What is Ecodrone

Ecodrone offers highly customizable products and services for the monitoring of marine and lake basins and fresh water. We provide solutions to all companies and public or private entities that need to automatically acquire data relating to the state of the water and the seabed including not only physical, chemical and meteorological data but also aspects relating to the biodiversity of the observed environments.
